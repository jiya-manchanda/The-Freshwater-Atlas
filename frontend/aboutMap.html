About the Project

The **Freshwater Atlas** is an innovative project designed to bring the critical importance of freshwater resources in Orange County, Florida, to life. Using interactive technology and data-driven insights, this atlas serves as a comprehensive educational tool to promote awareness and inspire conservation.

By focusing on key freshwater sites—including lakes, rivers, and springs—the map reveals their essential role in supporting ecosystems, human activity, and biodiversity. Through visual storytelling and dynamic exploration, the Freshwater Atlas empowers users with actionable knowledge about the distribution, significance, and challenges surrounding these resources.

Key Features

  • Interactive Exploration: Navigate through lakes, rivers, springs, and other freshwater bodies with rich data overlays.
  • Educational Insights: Discover water quality reports, conservation efforts, and wildlife habitats.
  • Real-World Impact: Understand the challenges of pollution, overuse, and restoration efforts in preserving these vital resources.

Our Vision

At its core, the Freshwater Atlas is about education and advocacy. We aim to:

  • Encourage stewardship of natural water resources among residents and policymakers.
  • Raise awareness of the interconnectedness between freshwater ecosystems and human activity.
  • Provide a user-friendly platform that translates complex data into actionable insights.

Data Sources

Our project integrates data from reliable and trusted sources to ensure accuracy and relevance. These include:

  • Orange County Water Atlas
  • St. Johns River Water Management District
  • USGS National Water Information System
  • Florida DEP Waterbody IDs (WBIDs)
  • Freshwater Research Center at Rollins
  • Local environmental reports and conservation studies

Technology

The Freshwater Atlas leverages the Google Maps API to create a dynamic, visually engaging experience. Through interactive layers and data visualization, the atlas provides a seamless and intuitive user interface that enhances learning and exploration.

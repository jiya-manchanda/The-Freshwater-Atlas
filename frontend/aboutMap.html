About the Project

The Freshwater Atlas is an interactive map designed to explore and highlight the vital freshwater resources of Orange County, Florida. This project is a culmination of research, data analysis, and design efforts to create an engaging tool that fosters a deeper understanding of the distribution, importance, and challenges surrounding these essential natural resources.

With a focus on key freshwater sites—including lakes, rivers, and springs—the map serves as a gateway to understanding their role in sustaining local ecosystems and supporting human activity. By providing insights into water quality, conservation efforts, and the interconnected nature of these resources, the atlas aims to empower users with knowledge about the critical importance of preserving these ecosystems.

Key Features

  • Explore Water Bodies: Interactive points and overlays for lakes, rivers, springs, and other key freshwater sites in Orange County.
  • Learn About Conservation: Information on water quality, wildlife habitats, pollution sources, and restoration initiatives.
  • Understand Interconnections: Visualize how freshwater resources support both environmental and human systems.

Our Vision

The Freshwater Atlas seeks to be more than just a map—it is an educational tool designed to foster appreciation and action. Whether you are a local resident, student, or environmental enthusiast, our goal is to inspire a greater sense of stewardship for these precious water resources and to raise awareness of the challenges they face, such as pollution, overuse, and habitat degradation.

Data Sources

To ensure accuracy and depth, this project incorporates data from trusted sources, including:

  • Orange County Water Atlas
  • St. Johns River Water Management District
  • USGS National Water Information System
  • Florida DEP Waterbody IDs (WBIDs)
  • Freshwater Research Center at Rollins
  • Local environmental reports and conservation studies

Technology

The Freshwater Atlas leverages the Google Maps API to create a visually engaging and interactive experience. Layers and data visualizations are dynamically integrated to provide an intuitive user interface that encourages exploration and discovery.
